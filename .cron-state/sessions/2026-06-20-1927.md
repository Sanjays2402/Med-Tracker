## Design highlights

- **Composition over redundancy.** `dose-time-suggest` composes
  quiet-hours + food-windows + existing-doses in one decision (first
  module to do so). `pill-cutter-plan` complements dose-rounding
  rather than duplicating its multi-strength search.
  `adverse-event-log` is the per-event companion to
  side-effect-correlation's corpus signal.
- **Isomorphic crypto.** `caregiver-share-token` runs in Node 18+
  AND the browser via `globalThis.crypto.subtle`, no `@types/node`
  dep. The new `asBufferSource` shim is the documented escape hatch
  for TS 5.7+'s stricter BufferSource typing.
- **Safety-first defaults.** Tighter deviation cap on pill splitting
  than on dose rounding (5% vs 10%). Constant-time signature compare
  on token verify. Major adverse events don't auto-escalate without
  a suspect medication in the proximity window.
